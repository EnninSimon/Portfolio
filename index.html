About Me

I Am Awesomme!

I am Boahene Ansomah Faustina. I am 18 years of age currently residing at Suhum in the Eastern Region of Ghana with my parents. I come from a family of 6.

I completed Krobo Girls Senior High School and had the passion to study IT at the tertiary level. I spend most of my days thinking of how I will give back to my community through technology when I grow.

The dream I have for my country and Africa as a whole is huge. My prayer is to make Africa a better place to live! #WomenInTech
